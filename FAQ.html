Order

How do I place an order ?
To place your order, follow the steps mentioned below:
  • Browse by article of your choice.
  • View details of the product, select your size and add to "cart".
  • Create an account or you may proceed as a guest user.
  • Enter your shipping address with pin code.
  • Please make sure that you check the total amount and products added to your cart before proceeding.
  • Next, select the payment option. (Net-Banking/Debit/Credit Card/Wallets or COD).
  • To complete the online payment, you will be redirected to our online payment partner payU Money. For COD, you will need to confirm the order by entering the OTP received on your mobile.
Once your payment is confirmed, your order will be placed successfully. You will receive a confirmation SMS & Email with your order details.

As soon as the order is confirmed, you will receive two emails:
  • Browse by article of your choice.
  • A mail confirming that you have received the order.
What taxes will be levied on the transactions ?
All our prices are inclusive of sales tax. Sales Tax is calculated based on the GST laws.
Are there any shipping charges ?
There are no shipping charges.
Do you deliver anywhere in India?
We deliver to most cities in India Please note: Currently we are not shipping internationally.

How do I clean my SPORTS-HUB shoes, apparel or accessories?
Apparels Please pay close attention to the care instructions provided with each article of SPORTS-HUB clothing. Many of our products should not be put in a dryer, for example, to prevent shrinkage. In general, turn your SPORTS-HUB clothes inside out and then wash and iron them to protect the printed graphics and embroidery from damage. Footwear Our high-performance shoes require little maintenance because they're tough and durable. By following these simple directions, you'll enjoy your SPORTS-HUB shoes even longer:
  • Clean your shoes immediately after use if they are exposed to mud or dirt.
  • Use your shoes only for their intended purpose.
  • Dry your shoes by removing the footbed and allowing them to air dry.
  • Pack your shoes with crumpled newspaper to help them retain their shape.
  • Never machine-wash or machine-dry your shoes.
  • Next, select the payment option. (Net-Banking/Debit/Credit Card/Wallets or COD).
  • Keep your shoes away from sources of extreme heat, such as radiators, dryers, or space heaters.

How can I place an exchange request at Sports-Hub stores?
If your product is purchased from a Sports-Hub retail stores, please address this matter with the store from which the item was originally purchased.
  • For exchange or claim, it is mandatory to have the article inspected by the store team.
  • Store team can only offer a exchange against article available at the store at the time of the exchange/claim being made.
  • Replacement against exchange/claims will be subject to availability of the same product. In case, the same product is not available at the store, then a replacement will be adjusted against the subsequent purchase at the same store.
  • The value of the replaced article will be equal to the original purchase price of the exchanged/claimed article, as mentioned in the Invoice, irrespective of the current price of the exchanged/claimed article, at the time of the exchange/claim being made.
  • If the value of the replaced article is less than the original value of the exchanged/claimed article, there will be no cash refund, or a credit note issued.
  • If the value of the replaced article is more than the original value, the store will recover the difference in cash/card against the sale.
  • No warranty/exchange for articles purchased on discount or during the sale period Carrying the Original Bill and the Product with the tags intact is mandatory
